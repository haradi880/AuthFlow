Privacy Policy

AuthFlow uses essential cookies for login sessions, CSRF protection, and remember-me authentication. Optional analytics or marketing cookies should only run after consent.

We store profile information, uploads, messages, notifications, follows, likes, bookmarks, and content you publish so the app can provide its social and portfolio features.

Email may be used for OTP verification, password reset, message/comment/follow/like notifications, and weekly digest preferences you control in settings.

Uploads are served as public assets when attached to public profiles, blogs, or projects. Direct messages are private to the participants and administrators should treat them as sensitive operational data.

Account export and deletion are planned self-service controls. Until then, users should contact the site operator for deletion/export requests.
